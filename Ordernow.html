How to Order

We make it simple and easy for you to order your favorite comics! Follow the steps below to place your order.

  • Browse through our collection on the homepage.
  • Take note of the comics you want to purchase.
  • Send us a direct message (DM) on Instagram or WhatsApp with the names of the comics you'd like to buy.
  • We will confirm availability, prices, and delivery details with you.
